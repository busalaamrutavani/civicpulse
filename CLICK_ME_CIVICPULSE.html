Election FAQ

Q: What ID do I need to vote?
A: It varies by state. Some require photo ID, others just a signature match.

Q: Can I vote by mail?
A: Most states offer some form of mail-in voting. Some require an excuse, while many are 'No-Excuse'.

Q: How do I find my polling place?
A: Use the CivicPulse assistant or check your state's voter portal.
